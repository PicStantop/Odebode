Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Property Valuation and why do I need one?

Property valuation is a professional assessment of a property's market value. It is essential for mortgage applications, insurance, financial reporting, taxation, and determining a fair price before buying or selling.

Are your surveyors NIESV and ESVARBON certified?

Yes. Seyi Odebode & Associates is a registered firm. Our lead surveyors are members of the Nigerian Institution of Estate Surveyors and Valuers (NIESV) and are duly registered by the Estate Surveyors and Valuers Registration Board of Nigeria (ESVARBON).

Which locations do you cover in Nigeria?

While our head office is located in Lagos Island, we provide professional real estate consultancy and valuation services to clients across all states in Nigeria.

How long does a typical valuation report take?

The timeline depends on the complexity of the asset, but standard residential valuations are typically completed within 3 to 5 working days after the physical inspection.
