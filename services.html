Our Professional Services

Valuation Report

Comprehensive valuation reports for diverse purposes including mortgages, insurance, financial reporting, mergers, and probate.

Property Management

Expert administration of residential and commercial properties. We handle tenant relations, rent collection, and lease administration to maximize your returns.

Real Estate Agency

Professional brokerage services for buying, selling, and leasing properties. We connect property owners with verified buyers and tenants across Nigeria.

Project Management

End-to-end oversight of real estate development projects. We ensure your construction projects are delivered on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards.

Facility Management

Integrated maintenance and operations of buildings and infrastructure. We ensure functionality, comfort, safety, and efficiency of your built environment.

Objective and unbiased professional opinion of the value of property rights, essential for taxation, investment analysis, and legal proceedings.

Property Investment Analysts and Advisors. We provide strategic insights, market research, and feasibility studies to guide high-yield real estate investments.
